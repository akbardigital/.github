## 👋 Who We Are

**Akbar Digital** is a passionate team of developers, designers, and tech enthusiasts. We specialize in crafting high-quality digital solutions that bridge the gap between business needs and technological innovation. 

Whether it's empowering local MSMEs (UMKM) to go digital, building robust SaaS products, or engineering complex custom systems for academic and enterprise research, we write code that matters.

### 🎯 Our Core Focus
* **🏢 B2B Solutions:** Premium Company Profiles, High-conversion Landing Pages, and custom CRM/POS systems.
* **🎓 Academic & R&D:** Custom system development, complex algorithm implementations, and secure data handling for final projects or research.
* **⚡ Modern Web Apps:** Scalable Single Page Applications (SPA) and real-time microservices.
